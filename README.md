Project Overview: 
Grap Smack is an omnidirectional adventure game that features a unique twist on the typical platformer: grappling mechanics! 
You are a monkey. One involved in a tragic accident, losing an arm and half your face. However, due to advanced alien technology, 
you have been reconstructed into a cyborg creature. One capable of saving the jungle from the mass infiltration of zombie monkeys? 
Can you reach and defeat the mastermind behind this **_brainrotted_** invasion?
